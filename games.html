My Favorite Games

Hello!

If you clicked on this link, that means you must be interested in learning about my top 10 favorite games of all time!

Let's get started with:

  • Minecraft: Minecraft is a sandbox video game developed by Mojang Studios. It allows players to build and explore virtual worlds made up of blocks. Players can gather resources, craft items, and create structures in a procedurally generated world. The game has various modes, including survival mode, where players must manage their health and hunger, and creative mode, where they have unlimited resources to build freely. Minecraft has gained immense popularity for its open-ended gameplay and has become a cultural phenomenon since its release in 2011.
  • Persona 5: Persona 5 is a role-playing style video game that takes place in modern (at the time) Japan, and features a story about rebellion and self-discovery through the eyes of a group of high school outcasts. Join Joker, and the rest of the phantom thieves as they liberate society from the stuck up adults who run the show, using entities known as personas, magical beings that are manifestations of the characters' inner selves. The game is known for it's stylish visuals, engaging story, and unique turn-based combat system. As well, Persona was the first game I've ever played that had a combat system known as the Baton pass, if you down an enemy, you can give one of your teammates a turn and hit them with a stronger attack, and if you build it up with all four characters, you can unleash an all powerful attack. Persona was one of the first RPGs i played that i thoroughly enjoyed (and while I usually enjoy most of the RPGs I played, Persona 5 is one of the few that I have actually finished)
  • Resident Evil: Resident Evil is a survival horror video game series developed by Capcom. It follows the story of characters dealing with zombie outbreaks and other supernatural threats. The series is known for its tense atmosphere and demanding gameplay.
